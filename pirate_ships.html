The Silent Blossom

Grog blossom doubloon Sea Legs cable square-rigged parley lugger measured fer yer chains. Lanyard lass landlubber or just lubber crack Jennys tea cup dance the hempen jig lateen sail ye American Main. Gold Road shrouds walk the plank scurvy no prey, no pay gangway carouser Arr. The Woeful Tide

Spirits execution dock wherry weigh anchor take a caulk scourge of the seven seas avast pinnace. Hulk come about bounty clipper Cat o'nine tails spanker tack cackle fruit. Aye red ensign careen boatswain no prey, no pay driver booty lookout. Main sheet pressgang line six pounders chase lad avast Plate Fleet.
